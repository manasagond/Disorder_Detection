Quick Guide

Stress

Meaning: Stress is a state of mental or emotional strain caused by challenging or demanding circumstances.

Causes: Work pressure, family issues, financial concerns, etc.

Detection Criteria: Restlessness, irritability, fatigue, headaches, muscle tension.

No Stress

Meaning: No stress refers to a calm and relaxed mental state without emotional strain.

Causes: Healthy work-life balance, positive mental habits, supportive relationships.

Detection Criteria: Calm demeanor, steady mood, physical relaxation.

Depressed

Meaning: Depression is a mood disorder characterized by persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities, and low energy.

Causes: Genetics, trauma, stress, chemical imbalances in the brain.

Detection Criteria: Loss of interest, fatigue, hopelessness, changes in sleep/appetite.

Not Depressed

Meaning: A state where the individual is mentally balanced and not experiencing symptoms of depression.

Causes: Emotional well-being, healthy lifestyle, fulfilling relationships.

Detection Criteria: Positive mood, balanced energy levels, interest in daily activities.

Anxiety

Meaning: Anxiety is a mental health condition characterized by excessive worry, fear, or nervousness.

Causes: Stressful situations, genetics, trauma, hormonal imbalances.

Detection Criteria:

Restlessness, racing thoughts, physical symptoms like sweating or increased heart rate.
